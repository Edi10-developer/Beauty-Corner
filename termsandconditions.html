Terms and Conditions

Appointments

When you are booking an appointment you will be agreeing cancellation/ no show policy. If you cancel an appointment with 48 hours or less notice you will need to pay 50% of the appointment price you were booked in for. If you do not show up for your appointment you will also have to pay 100% of the treatment price you were booked in for. Failure to pay no show fee will mean you will not be allowed to book in again.

Deposit

Due to a lot of cancellations new clients are required to pay a non refundable fee 50% of the appointment price you are booking in for. Appointment will be confirmed only when payment is received.

Timings

Please be on time and give yourself enough time to find the location. If you are 10-15 min late for your appointment I may cancel your appointment because I can't run over other client appointment time. It will be classed as no show and charged 50% fee of the appointment price. Please arrive max 10 min before your appointment. As a home based business I am allowed to have only one client in at the same time.

Cancellations

Cancellations within 48 hours will incur a charge of 50% of the service. No further appointments can be booked until the cancellation fee has been paid. If you want to swap appointments, I will require a new deposit.
